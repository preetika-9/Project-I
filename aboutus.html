*About Us*

Find job.com is the most innovative and second largest online job portal in Nepal.
Founded in 2008, over the past decade, Find job.com has become a prominent name
in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the portal is evident from the fact that
it has crossed the 3.4 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job
vacancies from leading companies on the site.
Find job.com connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate
profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology.
While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, Find job focuses
on the entire career growth of candidates. To this end, Find job has launched Learning
Napal’s largest career skills site for working professionals with over 500+ courses &
certifications.
As the industry shifts towards mobile, Find job.com is leading the transition and is
the fastest growing job portal on mobile devices, witnessing a 100% YOY growth in
mobile traffic and also offers on-the-go jobs through the Shine Job Search App.
Find job works closely to bridge the gap between talent & opportunities and offers
end-to-end recruitment solutions. Find Job Fair brings candidates and top employers
under one roof. While Shine HR Conclave brings top HR leaders to share insights on
latest trends, innovations & best practices in the HR industry. Find job also has a
large reach through its print product, Find Jobs – the Tuesday Job supplement of The
Himalayan Times– making it the only job portal with an integrated print and online
offering.
